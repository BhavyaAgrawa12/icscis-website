About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is the largest state of India, encompassing the vast stretches of the Thar Desert, grand palaces, and magnificent forts that reflect its glorious past. Known for its vibrant culture, colorful festivals, folk music, and deeply rooted traditions, the state showcases a rich royal heritage in every corner.

Its majestic architecture and lively cultural spirit make Rajasthan one of the most fascinating and culturally diverse regions of the country, attracting countless tourists from across the globe.

About Poornima University

Poornima University (PU) is a state private University in Jaipur, India established by an act of the Rajasthan legislature. PU was established in 2012 with a vision to create a knowledge-based society with scientific temper, team spirit and dignity of labour to face global competition and challenges.

PU is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) and 12(B) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 (1) of the UGC Act, 1956. Its architecture program is approved by the Council of Architecture (CoA) and the hotel management program is approved by NCHMCT.

Currently, PU has more than 8000+ students from all over the country enrolled in nine faculties and fourteen departments of UG/PG and PhD Programs in various areas such as Management, Engineering, Computer Application Sciences, Architecture, Design, Public Health and Hotel Management, etc.

The university is committed to fostering research and innovation, and this conference is part of its mission to promote cutting-edge research in sustainable computing and intelligent systems.

About the FCE Department

Vision

To create a leading center of excellence in computing education, research, and innovation that empowers learners with advanced technological skills, nurtures problem-solving abilities, and contributes to the development of a smart, secure, and sustainable digital society.

Mission

  • 1. To provide an outcome-based, industry-relevant curriculum that enhances technical competence and lifelong learning.
  • 2. To promote innovation, research culture, and technological entrepreneurship among students and faculty.
  • 3. To strengthen collaborations with industry, research organizations, and global institutions for knowledge sharing and skill enhancement.
  • 4. To develop ethical, skilled, and socially responsible computing professionals capable of contributing to national and global technological growth.

The Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering at Poornima University is a dynamic hub of technological learning, innovation, and research excellence. It is committed to shaping future-ready professionals equipped with the skills and mindset required to excel in an increasingly digital world.

With a strong emphasis on emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, IoT, Blockchain, Data Analytics, DevOps, and Full-Stack Development, the Faculty of CSE integrates scientific knowledge with real-world application.

By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, research engagement, and ethical computing practices, the Faculty of CSE plays a pivotal role in driving innovation within the university. It aspires to cultivate skilled engineers who can address modern challenges, adapt to evolving technologies, and lead with integrity in global digital environments.

About the R&D Cell

Vision

To establish a robust mechanism for developing and strengthening the research ecosystem within university to reshape industries and elevate society for the benefit of the world using transformative innovation and advancing knowledge.

Mission

  • 1. To create an enabling environment for enhancing research productivity.
  • 2. To encourage collaboration among industry, government, community-based organizations, and agencies.
  • 3. To facilitate greater access to research through mobilization of resources and funding.

Poornima University's Research and Development (R&D) cell is a vibrant centre of research and innovation, dedicated to advancing cutting-edge research in a variety of subjects and pushing the boundaries of knowledge for promoting advanced research across diverse fields. The R&D cell works on challenges like healthcare, education, environmental sustainability, and social fairness with an emphasis on interdisciplinary approaches and ethical research practices.
