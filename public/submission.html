Paper Submission

Submit your research papers for ICSCIS 2026

Paper Submission Guidelines

Instructions:

  • • Kindly use the Full Paper Template provided by the journal/website (in the Download tab) for formatting your paper.
  • • The paper should be original and should not have been submitted anywhere/published else or be under review for any journal or other conferences.
  • • The plagiarism should be less than 10%.
  • • The full article must be submitted as a MS Word file in DOC or DOCX format.
  • • Before submitting your paper, please ensure that the English used is clear, concise and coherent. This is especially important if English is not your first language.

NOTE:

At least one author must register for inclusion. Only registered & presented papers will be processed for publication. No paper to have more than six authors.

How to Submit?

Perspective authors shall submit full-length manuscripts via the submission link.

Submit Your Paper

Authors will receive notification from the conference once the Technical Program Committee has reviewed the manuscript. . The committee accepts only full-length papers for review not the abstracts.

Review Process & Presentation

All papers with a similarity score of less than 10% will undergo a double-blind review. The technical committee of the conference will review the papers, and the authors will be notified of the paper status, which can be one of the following:

  • • Accepted for oral presentation
  • • Accepted with minor corrections for Oral Presentation
  • • Rejected

Full Paper Submission up to July 31, 2026
Notification of Acceptance August 20, 2026
Revised Paper Submission (if accepted) August 31, 2026
Early Bird Registration September 5, 2026
Late Registration September 15, 2026
Conference Dates September 25-26, 2026
