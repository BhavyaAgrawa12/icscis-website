About Rajasthan

Rajasthan is the largest state of India, encompassing the vast stretches of the Thar Desert, grand palaces, and magnificent forts that reflect its glorious past. Known for its vibrant culture, colorful festivals, folk music, and deeply rooted traditions, the state showcases a rich royal heritage in every corner.

Its majestic architecture and lively cultural spirit make Rajasthan one of the most fascinating and culturally diverse regions of the country, attracting countless tourists from across the globe.

About Poornima University

Poornima University (PU) is a state private University in Jaipur, India established by an act of the Rajasthan legislature. PU was established in 2012 with a vision to create a knowledge-based society with scientific temper, team spirit and dignity of labour to face global competition and challenges.

PU is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) and 12(B) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 (1) of the UGC Act, 1956. Its architecture program is approved by the Council of Architecture (CoA) and the hotel management program is approved by NCHMCT.

Currently, PU has more than 8000+ students from all over the country enrolled in nine faculties and fourteen departments of UG/PG and PhD Programs in various areas such as Management, Engineering, Computer Application Sciences, Architecture, Design, Public Health and Hotel Management, etc.

The university is committed to fostering research and innovation, and this conference is part of its mission to promote cutting-edge research in sustainable computing and intelligent systems.

About NITTTR, Bhopal

National Institute of Technical Teachers' Training and Research (NITTTR), Bhopal is a premier institution established in 1965 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, for teacher training and improving the overall quality of the Technical Education System across the country.

The Institute has developed expertise through continuous research and development in areas such as competency development in emerging technologies, subject matter enhancement, classroom and laboratory-based learning, industry-institute internships, management of technical institutions, personality development, entrepreneurship development, examination reforms, and student assessment.

It also specializes in instructional resource development, multimedia and video production, educational project planning and management, community development initiatives, and evaluation of programs and projects within the technical education ecosystem.

In 2003, the Government of India upgraded the Institute‚Äôs mandate, enabling it to function as a national-level resource institution beyond regional boundaries. With modern infrastructure and updated resources, NITTTR continues to support quality enhancement in technical education through strategic collaboration and networking with centers of excellence.

About RCAT

Rajasthan Centre of Advanced Technology (R-CAT) is formed under the Society Registration Act, 1958 and is managed under the aegis of the Department of Information Technology & Communication (DoIT&C), Government of Rajasthan (GoR). The R-CAT campus in Jaipur is developed at the Soochna Kendra Campus, opposite SMS Hospital.

R-CAT has been established to enhance the employability of engineering and technology students as well as youth of the state who aspire to get trained and absorbed into the emerging technologies industry.

The center has developed partnerships with leading technology OEMs and training partners including Apple, Adobe, Autofina Robotics, Cisco, EC Council, Microsoft, Oracle, Phytec, Red Hat, SAS, and VMware to offer globally recognized courses and certifications in advanced and emerging IT technologies.

Most of these programs are open to students with a STEM background. Additionally, selected creative programs in digital designing are accessible to candidates who have completed high school education.

The courses are offered in high-demand domains such as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI-ML), Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality (AR/VR), Big Data Analytics, Robotics, Quantum Computing, Digital Media Editing, and Graphic Design.

Through strategic industry collaborations and future-focused training initiatives, R-CAT plays a pivotal role in strengthening the state‚Äôs technical workforce and fostering innovation-driven growth.
